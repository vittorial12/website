ABOUT ME

Name: Vittoria Laudando

Hello and welcome! My name is Vittoria and I am currently a Senior at The University of Illinois at Chicago. I am currently the Treasurer of WICS at UIC, which stands for Women In Computer Science and a student mentor for Girls Who Code! WICS is an all inclusive club who aim to bring women in computer science together while also empowering and supporting all students in Computer Science. Girls Who Code aims is also an all inclusive club who aims to support and increase the number of women in computer science by targeting high school students allowing them to become better familiarized with computer science. Some hobbies I enjoy to do in my free time are swimming, painting, playing tennis, baking, and playing video games. I am also a very talkative person and always enjoy meeting new people and I have two adorable Egyptian Mau kittens :)
